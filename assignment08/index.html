How to make a Beer Dye table thats ready to play

Materials

  • 4ft-8ft plywood sheet

  • 2 sawhorses

  • 2 dice

  • spray paint

  • 4 solo cups

    • "

  • beer/drink

Building instructions

  1. Spray paint a line down the center of the 4ft-8ft sheet creating two 4ft-4ft squares on the table
  2. Let paint dry
  3. Lift the plywood on to the two sawhorses so the plywood doesnt wobble creating a table
  4. Place cups in each corner and fill with drink
  5. Play Beer Dye
