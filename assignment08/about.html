How to play Beer Dye

Players needed

  • 4

Games Rules

  • Two player's stand at each side of the table as a team
  • Player's toss the dye roughly 10ft above the table and try to land it on the opisite teams side and bounce off the table to the ground resulting in a point
  • If the die hits the line it is short and isnt counted
  • Player's being tossed at try to catch the dye after it bounces off the table and before it hits the ground stopping the other team from scoring
  • If you hit the opisite teams cup and the dye hits the ground that is 2 points
  • If you land the dye in the opisite teams cup they must finish their drink and you get 3 points
  • Players finish their drinks when 4 points are scored against them
  • The game is played to 12 when opisite teams scores add up to 12 you switch sides
