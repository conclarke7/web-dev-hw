Cooking Chicken Tenders

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Breading
  • Eggs
  • Seasoning

Directions

  1. Crack eggs then whip them
  2. Coat chicken in eggs then in breading and seasoning
  3. You will want to cook at 425 degrees for 30-40 min
  4. Take out and after cooling for 5 min enjoy!
